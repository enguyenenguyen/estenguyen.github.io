My presentation

Clothes, fashion, style including streetwear fascinates me. My curiosity particularly attracts me to clothing brands independent, their new designs or their new releases. My first occupation is to find shops and brands independent, often in streetwear style, and to be interested in their design and their ways to get there or simply find clothes I like to get them.

The system of these brands is also attractive, indeed their is special because it works in the form of drop, due to the fact that they have limited stocks due to their notoriety. This system seduces me even more than traditional customs because few people can get the same items as me, which makes some rarer pieces.

What is a drop? It is the opening or temporary sale of products, articles or collections because they are in limited edition. Generally, these are announced on social networks or by ads and the opening date varies depending on the drops, it can be 10 minutes or several weeks.

I find it exciting to create a brand in its entirety, invent, draw, realize design with for example photoshop in looking for what is not yet done on the market and what is what we still do not see or little, find and choose the right suppliers, decide on a production and storage location for products, seeking funding, defining the identity of the brand, name, logo, create an online store and finally put in place a communication and advertising strategy.
